About

“Island” is a Korean fantasy-hero drama set on Jeju Island about three individuals – Won Min-Ho (Lee Da Hee), Van (Kim Nam-Gil), and Johan (Cha Eun-Woo) – fighting against a greater evil set to destroy the world.
